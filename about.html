Who is Carrie Qian?

Hello,  I am a video content maker, mainly focus on stop motion content. Now I am based in Lund, Sweden. Originally from Shanghai, China.
In 2018, I have started my Stop Motion Study Group in Studieförbundet Vuxenskolan in Malmö. I share my experiences and knowledge of Stop Motion, to teach the beginner from scratch, it was a great time, and since I have not stopped on making content. I find it it is such a great tool to express myself. I have couple of on-going projects myself , to tell stories about my family and myself, using stop motion , illustration, paper cutting skills, hopefully it can be launched by year 2022. For the past few years, I have made some Stop Motion tutorials on Chinese social media.  It leads me into a  stop motion design project currently is on going in Shanghai. I also do video editing, sound effect recording, all sorts of content making.  Apart from the content making, I am currently studying programming at Code Institute.

What are Carrie's tools?

Stop Motion Studio app, Adobe (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop), Procreate, Nomad, Final Cut pros, Videoleap, Keynote.
