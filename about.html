Who is Carrie Qian?

Hello,  I am a video content maker. Now I am based in Lund, Sweden. Originally from Shanghai, China. Stop Motion is the most common content I make.
In 2018, I have started my Stop Motion Study Group in Studieförbundet Vuxenskolan in Malmö. I shared my experiences and knowledges of Stop Motion, to teach the beginners from scratch, it was a great time. Since then I have not stopped making content. I find it is such a great tool to express myself.
I have a couple of ongoing projects myself, to tell stories about my family and myself, using stop motion, illustration, paper cutting skills. Hopefully, it can be launched by the year 2022.
For the past few years, I have made some Stop Motion tutorials on Chinese social media.  It leads me into a  stop motion design project currently is ongoing in Shanghai. I also do video editing, sound effect recording, all sorts of content making.  Apart from content making, I am currently studying programming at Code Institute.

What are Carrie's tools?

Stop Motion Studio app, Adobe (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop), Procreate, Nomad, Final Cut pros, Videoleap, Keynote.
